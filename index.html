Our Journey

Caravan Custom Canvas

Caravan Custom canvas WA, is the best place for custom-made canvas and PVC products specific to their customers’ needs. We specialize in the industrial sewing and repair of canvas and PVC goods. Our business has been built primarily on word of mouth recommendations, as we are fully committed to customer service and high-quality workmanship. Caravan Custom Canvas have a large canvas team which allows us to make a comprehensive range of products including awning and walls; shade walls; caravan covers and camper re-canvasing, ute canopies, caravan annexes and much more. Furthermore, we are manufacturing any custom-made canvas and PVC products that are specifically designed as per the customer requirements. If you are looking for canvas or PVC sewing in your area or anywhere in Perth, please do not hesitate to call 0424 848 521, We will work as a team and find the best possible solution together.

Canvas & PVC Goods Repairs

We offer a vast range of repair and alteration services for a varied range of products. Ranging from repair of camper trailer canvas to small tent zipper repairs, from patching small tears to replacing complete sections, Caravan Custom Canvas offer extensive experience and expertise, in doing all types of repairs and replacement.
